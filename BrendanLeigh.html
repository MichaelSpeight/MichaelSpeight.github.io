Brendan "Blockhead" Leigh

Another new addition this year coming all the way from Manchester. He has had an illustrious career in the UK playing for the likes of Nuneaton Borough and Chorley FC and he is seeing out his final playing days here in Wellington. With a deft touch and composure on the ball he has been brought in to link the midfield to the strikers on days it is incredibly hard to do so. He has brought all his Football Manager tacticle knowledge which Powershop FC are duely going to exploit.
