Bryan "I only play in midfield" Lennon

Another fresh import that originates from the far flung shores of Ireland. Bryan is a midfield maestro that conducts the tempo of each game and contributes to the goal tally too. He is known to throw his toys out of the pram when asked to play in a non attacking role but we wouldn't want it any other way, assertiveness is what we require on and off the pitch.
