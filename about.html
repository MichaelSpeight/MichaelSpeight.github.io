About

Powershop FC was formed in 2014 by a group of guys that wanted to play competitive football whilst enjoying the social aspect of the game. Daniel Johnson our sponsorship manager raised enough funds through a major sponsorship deal to pay for brand new kit, new footballs and training gear.

We had a great first season finishing 4th in the league and going unbeaten at home all season, in fact we are still unbeaten to this day! The start of this season has a been a great one and we currently sit 3rd.
