Andrew "Crazy eyes" Fyers

Andrew is one of our longest serving players and he is now our player manager. With a natural left foot he is deadly on set pieces in or around the box. He is advancing in years and accumulating more gray hairs (pressure of management?) but he is a key player for us in defence. With his calm composed nature his distribution is 2nd to none. He is a bit of a light weight when it comes to drinking and he is known to lose control of his eye balls after 2 pints.
