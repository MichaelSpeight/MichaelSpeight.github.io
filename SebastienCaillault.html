Sebastien "The machine" Caillault

Originating from the lower French leagues he has plied his trade with several French international players such as Patrice Evra and Thierry Henry. A no nonsense approach to his game added to his lightning pace he is a natural winger. He has returned this season in his best shape and the results are paying off on the field.
